CHICKEN ADOBO

Savor the rich, comforting flavors of classic Filipino chicken adobo—tender chicken simmered in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices until perfectly juicy and flavorful. Every bite is a delicious balance of savory, tangy, and aromatic goodness that feels like home.

Ingredients:

  • CHICKEN
  • SOY SAUCE
  • WHITE VINEGAR
  • GARLIC
  • DRIED BAY LEAVES
  • WHOLE BLACK PEPPERCORNS
  • SUGAR
  • WATER
  • COOKING OIL

Steps:

  1. Marinate The Chicken
  2. Pan Fry The Chicken
  3. Simmer
  4. Add Vinegar and Finish
